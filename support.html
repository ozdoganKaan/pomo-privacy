Privacy Policy for Pomio

Last updated: January 2025

Pomio (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a productivity and time-management mobile application that helps users plan their goals and manage Pomodoro sessions. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address

b. Device Information

  • Push notification token

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Manage your account
  • Display your profile
  • Send notifications you choose to receive

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

5. Account Deletion

If you want your data deleted, you can contact us at:

support@pomio.org

6. Children’s Privacy

Pomio is not intended for children under 13.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

support@pomio.org
